SGP is a Gadget Protection & Extended Warranty Program from SuperGeeks that covers all your gadgets and consumer electronics.

How It Works

To enjoy in the SuperGeeks Protection Program, you can signup up for an accompanying plan on purchase of your device. During the duration of the program, SuperGeeks will fix or replace your device if it goes bad

What devices are covered?

SuperGeeks Protection covers:

  • Mobile Phones & Tablets
  • Laptops / Desktops
  • Printers
  • Home Electronics
  • Smart TVs
  • Air Conditioners

What does SGP plan cover

SGP plans covers both hardware and software issues, which might incldue:

  • Accidental Damage
  • Irrepairable Damage
  • Hardware Malfunction
  • Software Malfunctions
  • Power Issues
  • Faulty Parts

  • System Failure
  • + More
